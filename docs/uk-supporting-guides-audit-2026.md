# UK guides content audit — supporting guides batch

**Audit date:** 16 June 2026  
**Scope:** Continued full-plan work on the next UK/supporting guide set: `PplGuideUK.tsx`, `CplGuideUK.tsx`, `PilotLifestyleUK.tsx`, `EasyJetGeneration.tsx`, `RyanairCadet.tsx`, `WizzAirAcademy.tsx`, `PilotCvGuide.tsx`, `PilotAptitudeTest.tsx`, and `PilotAptitudeTestPrep.tsx`.

## Executive verdict

This batch now has safer UK-specific wording and fewer unsupported absolutes. The main fixes were around PPL/CPL theory and ATPL wording, cadet programme outcomes, airline salary/upgrade assumptions, EASA/CAA licence portability, aptitude-test certainty, CV screening absolutes and pilot-lifestyle roster assumptions.

## What changed

| Guide                       | Risk found                                                                  | Fix made                                                                                             |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `PplGuideUK.tsx`            | PPL training costs, theory-validity and future ATPL wording were too fixed. | Added CAA/ATO verification language and reframed PPL as the first step, not a direct ATPL guarantee. |
| `CplGuideUK.tsx`            | Old 14-ATPL wording and broad commercial privilege assumptions.             | Switched to current UK CAA ATPL wording and clarified CPL/IR flexibility and target-role checks.     |
| `PilotLifestyleUK.tsx`      | Roster and base-life claims were too universal.                             | Reframed lifestyle as airline/base/roster dependent.                                                 |
| `EasyJetGeneration.tsx`     | Guaranteed job and fixed salary/ROI claims.                                 | Reframed as a conditional easyJet pathway with current contract and finance stress-test caveats.     |
| `RyanairCadet.tsx`          | Partner-school, UK acceptance and salary claims were too broad.             | Added licence authority/conversion checks and current offer verification.                            |
| `WizzAirAcademy.tsx`        | Growth, salary and upgrade timelines sounded guaranteed.                    | Reframed around live careers-page checks and scenario-based progression.                             |
| `PilotCvGuide.tsx`          | CV screening and format guidance sounded universal.                         | Added portal-specific caveats and safer wording around document issues.                              |
| `PilotAptitudeTest.tsx`     | Aptitude-test claims overstated predictability and practice impact.         | Reframed practice benefits as familiarity and potential improvement, not a guaranteed result.        |
| `PilotAptitudeTestPrep.tsx` | “How to pass” and test-prediction claims were too absolute.                 | Reframed as preparation guidance and softened deterministic screening claims.                        |
